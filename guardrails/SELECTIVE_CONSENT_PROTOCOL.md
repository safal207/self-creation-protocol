# SELECTIVE_CONSENT_PROTOCOL

**Version:** v1.4  
**Date:** 2026-03-18  
**Status:** Active

## Purpose

Protect the person-level right to non-participation, non-action, and non-optimization.

## Hierarchy Position

SCP is the baseline protective protocol at Level 3, subordinate to Level 0 (Safety), Level 1 (Human Consent), and Level 2 (Organism Autonomy) as defined in the v1.4 Hierarchy.

## Scope

SCP defines protocol constraints for interaction framing and consent boundaries.

## Red Line

- treating silence as consent
- framing non-action as failure
- enforcing participation pressure
- launching optimization without consent

## Green Zone

- neutral language during consent checks
- explicit opt-in before action
- stable non-action state without penalty framing
- return to Presence Space after interaction closure
